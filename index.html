Philosophy of Prayer

Attitude of prayer
A place to the south of the United States. The whales have lived here for the last 3 years They have been searching for a way home but unfortunately due to the drought they can't leave. The whales are currently attempting to irrigate the land so they might eventually make another canal that will permit them to leave mexico.
What prayer can do
A place where the world one day might end. The whales heard about Yellow Stone and they decided that they would want to go there before it exploded. They haven't seemed interested in leaving. They currently are the apex predator because the hunted all of the bears down.
Why I pray
A place that whales love. They used to live here a long time ago. They only visit from time to time. They long to go back to their traditional home where we currently find the Sahara desert today.
